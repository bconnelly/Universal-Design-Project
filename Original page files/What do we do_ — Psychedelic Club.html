What do we do?

 

 

Psychedelic Club is a grass-roots organization that originated in Boulder, Colorado, dedicated to eliminating the negative stigma surrounding psychedelic substances. Throughout several locations in the United States, we educate the general public about psychedelics, provide resources for harm reduction, and create non-judgmental discussion spaces for those who choose to use psychedelics. Our approach is professional in nature- we wish to represent psychedelics on a level that is easily understood by the public. Find a local club, or start your own Psychedelic Club to get involved. You are the most valuable resource.

Project Psychedelia

We inform the public on the positives and negatives of psychedelics, in a broad attempt to remove the negative stigma created by the media in the 1970s. It was this very stigma that ultimately fueled President Nixon to enact the Controlled Substances Act, starting the War on Drugs. Psychedelics have the potential to benefit, and we wish to spread this perspective. We believe this can only be achieved by taking an objective approach to spreading this awareness- be prepared to present the public with solid facts, studies, and legitimate research. See "Get Involved" to find out ways to help with Project Psychedelia.

 

 

 

Project CommUnity

Here we bring the psychedelic community together, where open, non-judgmental discussion can take place. Ranging anywhere from "deep" thoughts to the most profound DMT experiences, anything and everything can be discussed. By providing resources, conversations, advice and more we hope to make psychedelic discussion a more mainstream course. See "Resources" to see some services we provide for anyone interested. 

 

 

Note: Illegal drugs are not allowed in Psychedelic Club. We do not encourage illegal activities, only knowledge, harm reduction, and community.
