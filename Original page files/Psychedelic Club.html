Spreading perspective.

Welcome to Psychedelic Club.

 

 

We spread awareness about the positive impact psychedelics could have on society. 

 

We emphasize the facts by spreading knowledge through a professional standpoint. Driven by studies through research organizations such as MAPS (Multidisciplinary Association for Psychedelic Studies), Johns Hopkins Medicine, and the Naropa Alliance for Psychedelic Studies, we hope to point out how psychedelics could be used for PTSD, depression, personality growth, and spirituality. We welcome anyone to help, but thrive on the passionate ones.
